About Me

Welcome to my corner of the universe! I'm a passionate astrophysics student dedicated to understanding the cosmos and its mysteries. My journey in astronomy began with a simple question: "What's out there?" and has evolved into a deep fascination with stellar evolution, exoplanets, and the fundamental forces that shape our universe.

Currently pursuing my degree in astrophysics, I'm actively engaged in research projects and constantly seeking new ways to contribute to our understanding of the cosmos. When I'm not studying distant galaxies or analyzing spectroscopic data, you can find me at the local observatory or working on computational simulations.
